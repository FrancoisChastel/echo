On attention

2026.04.11 · 800 words · 4 min · attention · craft

What we keep, and what we let pass — most of a life is the second category. Attention is the small portion of the world we agree to receive in full, and to which we then owe something.

The owing is the part nobody talks about. We treat attention as a finite resource to be allocated, but it is also a kind of debt. To attend to something is to accept being changed by it.

A friend who reads everything I write said once: "you keep returning to this." And it was true. I had not noticed because each return felt fresh — but stepping back, I could see the pattern. The same idea, approached at different angles, year after year.
