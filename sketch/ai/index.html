— ai gallery

Five visitor-facing AI moves. None is a chatbot. Each does one thing, then steps back. The AI never speaks. It surfaces what is already written, slowly, and one piece at a time.

  1. 01 · echo continues

    The Echo Continues

    Scroll past the human-written end of an essay. The corpus continues — paragraph after paragraph, in your voice, marked clearly as echo. As long as you keep scrolling, the work refracts beyond itself.
  2. 02 · drift

    The Drift

    At the foot of every post, one invitation: drift →. Click takes you to a single AI-chosen resonant other post. Not three. Not a list. One continuation, then another, then another. Reading by felt thread.
  3. 03 · echo chamber

    The Echo Chamber

    A single page at /echo. Speak any phrase. The corpus pauses, then types back the one sentence from inside the work that resonates with what you brought. Source linked. No history.
  4. 04 · marginal echoes

    The Marginal Echoes

    While reading any post, faint italic quotes from older posts surface in the right margin as you scroll. Not "related articles". Actual rhymes between paragraphs and earlier work. AI-found at publish time, cached, quiet.
  5. 05 · echo garden

    The Echo Garden

    A single page that is just the corpus, as a constellation. Each post a dot in its echo color, arranged not by date but by emotional similarity. Drift across the field; click any dot to read. Navigation by feeling, not catalogue.
  6. 06 · endless echo · live

    The Endless Echo

    Scroll past the human-written end and keep scrolling — forever. The corpus "notices" when you finish reading and begins typing on its own; every paragraph after waits for a scroll. As depth grows the prose shrinks into fragments, brackets, found phrases, glitched echoes, then single characters. Every word is corpus-stitched. Zero LLM inference at runtime.
  7. 06b · endless echo · rendered at depth 150

    The Endless Echo — depth 150

    The "post-mortem" view. Every paragraph the live demo would generate across 150 iterations, pre-rendered as a static page. Lets you evaluate the whole journey — prose → fragments → underground → found footage → dissolution — without the 20-minute scroll. Deterministic seed: same output every reload.
